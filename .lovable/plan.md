

## Zigma Global Environ Solutions — Website Plan

### Design Direction
- **Color scheme**: Deep green (#1B5E20) + white + dark charcoal — conveying sustainability and industrial strength
- **Style**: Modern, clean, professional with bold hero sections, smooth scroll animations, and industrial imagery
- **Typography**: Strong headings with clean body text

### Pages & Structure

#### 1. **Home Page**
- Full-width hero with tagline "Just Clean It" and a CTA to explore products/contact
- Stats counter section: 20M+ tons processed, 2500+ acres reclaimed, key numbers
- Quick overview cards for Services (Waste Management, Landfill Remediation, Machinery)
- Featured products carousel with machinery images from the PPTX
- Client testimonials / trust bar

#### 2. **About Us**
- Company overview, mission, vision
- Partnership with Blue Planet
- Environmental impact and sustainability commitment
- Team or leadership section placeholder

#### 3. **Services Page**
- Three service cards with detail sections:
  - Waste Management
  - Landfill Remediation  
  - Machinery Manufacturing
- Each with description, key benefits, and relevant imagery

#### 4. **Products Page (Machinery Division)**
- Product grid with cards for each machine, using actual images from the PPTX:
  - **Trommel Machines** — with specs and features from the presentation
  - **Conveyors** — all types, 500mm-2400mm range
  - **Disc Screen Separator** — high volume screening
  - **Air Density Separator (ADS)** — pressure-resistant design
  - **Overband Magnetic Separator (OBMS)** — self-cleaning, permanent magnets
  - **Shredders** (placeholder)
  - **Shredder Blades** (placeholder)
- Each product card expands to show full specs and feature bullet points from the PPTX

#### 5. **Projects / Case Studies**
- Achievement stats with animated counters
- Project showcase cards with before/after placeholders
- Key milestones timeline

#### 6. **Technology & Innovation**
- Patented technologies overview
- How Zigma's innovations improve efficiency
- Process flow diagrams (placeholder visuals)

#### 7. **Contact Page**
- Contact form (opens email client)
- Phone, email, address details
- Embedded map placeholder
- Office locations (local & global)

### Navigation
- Sticky top navbar with logo + 7 nav items (Home, About, Services, Products, Projects, Technology, Contact)
- Mobile hamburger menu
- Footer with quick links, contact info, social media icons

### Images
All product images from the uploaded PPTX will be extracted and used on the Products page (Trommel, Conveyors, Disc Screen, ADS, OBMS machines).
